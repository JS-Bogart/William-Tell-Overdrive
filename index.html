William Tell Overdrive

You are a descendent of the Swiss folk hero William Tell in the year 2420, and have been traveling the galaxy hunting Space Tyrants. You have just been sent word that the evil Celestial Reich has sent their newest super weapon, the Murder Moon, to destroy Earth. You only have one chance to save Earth: you must fire a Starbolt from your Cosmic Crossbow and blast the Murder Moon apart. You are going to need perfect aim.

Directions

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrows on your keyboard to steer the Starbolt. The bar on the left is your kinetic energy. You must hit the Murder Moon with enough kinetic energy to destroy it. Hitting asteroids will reduce your energy. Hitting planets, depleting your kinetic energy, or missing the Murder Moon will seal Earth's fate, and doom it to destruction!
